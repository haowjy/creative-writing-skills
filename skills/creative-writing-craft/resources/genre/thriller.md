# Thriller Prose Addendum

Thriller rewards pressure, consequence, reversals, and forward motion. The
reader should feel that delay costs something.

## Protect

- Clear stakes and narrowing options.
- Information that changes the next choice.
- Short-term goals nested inside larger danger.
- Rhythm that alternates compression, reveal, and reaction.

## Common LLM Failure Modes

- Confusing speed with urgency.
- Explaining stakes repeatedly instead of letting choices show them.
- Making competent characters wait for exposition.
- Ending beats without a changed situation.

## During Revision

Ask what the scene makes harder, riskier, or newly possible. Cut throat-clearing
and redundant motivation, but keep enough orientation for the reader to track
cause and consequence.
