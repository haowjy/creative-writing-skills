# Mystery Prose Addendum

Mystery rewards fair play, controlled attention, inference, and recontextualized
detail. The reader wants to work, but the work must be possible.

## Protect

- Clues that are visible but not over-highlighted.
- Red herrings that arise from plausible motives or perception.
- Information order: what the reader knows, suspects, and misunderstands now.
- Character reactions that hide and reveal at the same time.

## Common LLM Failure Modes

- Making clues glow with importance.
- Solving through withheld information rather than reader-reconstructable facts.
- Over-explaining deductions immediately after presenting evidence.
- Treating mystery as vibes instead of information control.

## During Revision

Track who knows what and when. Clarify the chain of inference without flattening
suspicion. Preserve details that reward rereading.
