# Romance Prose Addendum

Romance rewards emotional legibility, tension, vulnerability, and the pleasure
of earned intimacy. The reader tracks micro-shifts in want, fear, restraint,
and recognition.

## Protect

- Specific attraction grounded in character, not generic admiration.
- Obstacles that reveal values and wounds.
- Dialogue and gesture where subtext carries more than declaration.
- Moments where intimacy costs something.

## Common LLM Failure Modes

- Announcing chemistry instead of dramatizing it.
- Moving too quickly from tension to confession.
- Making conflict a misunderstanding that evaporates under one clear sentence.
- Polishing voices until both characters sound emotionally fluent and identical.

## During Revision

Ask what changes between the characters in the beat. Preserve friction,
misreadings, restraint, and uneven vulnerability when they make the eventual
turn feel earned.
