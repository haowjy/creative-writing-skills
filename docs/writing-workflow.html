Workflow overview

The workflow keeps creative authority with the author, coordination with muse, production with one writer, adversarial judgment in separate review contexts, and story memory in the knowledge layer.

Source links

Relevant snippet

Why one writer?

Voice continuity is fragile. The system collapses drafts, revisions, bridges, alternate takes, and polish into one writer, then changes mode through progressive guidance.

Why separate reviewers?

Critique, felt reader response, and continuity checking are different cognitive lanes. Fresh context gives better adversarial distance than asking the writer to self-grade.

Why fat skills?

Agents stay small so their role survives compaction. Skills carry reusable craft method: prose, scenes, critique, reader reward, staffing, and knowledge routing.
