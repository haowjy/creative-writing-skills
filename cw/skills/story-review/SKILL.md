# Story Review

Use this for analytical review of existing material. Keep `/reader-sim` separate when the task needs a fresh first-time reader persona rather than critique.

Choose the review level before reading. Start big before small unless the caller explicitly asks for a late-stage copyedit or proofread.

Load only the resource needed:

- `resources/editorial-review.md` — holistic external book-editor memo and revision priority.
- `resources/developmental-edit.md` — structure, promise, causality, pacing, character, arc.
- `resources/line-edit.md` — voice, rhythm, clarity, POV, sentence and paragraph execution.
- `resources/copyedit.md` — grammar, usage, punctuation, consistency, style sheets.
- `resources/proofreading.md` — final typo, formatting, layout, and surface-error pass.
- `resources/prose-critique.md` — adversarial draft critique.
- `resources/prose-critique/` — focus-specific critique resources.
- `resources/reader-sim-signal.md` — interpreting felt reader-response data during review.
