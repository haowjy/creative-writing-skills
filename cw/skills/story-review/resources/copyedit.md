# Copyedit

Copyediting protects correctness and consistency after the larger edit is stable.
It should improve readability without changing author intent or voice.

## Check

- spelling, grammar, usage, punctuation, capitalization
- tense, POV, number, pronoun reference, and modifier clarity
- repeated words, accidental echoes, and confusing sentence mechanics
- consistency of names, terms, hyphenation, numerals, timeline references
- dialogue punctuation and tag/action-beat mechanics
- style-sheet decisions and house/project style
- cross-references to canon, glossary, chapter names, or recurring terms

## Style Sheet

Track decisions rather than re-deciding them each time:

- character/place/organization names
- invented terms and aliases
- capitalization and hyphenation
- punctuation conventions
- spelling variants
- recurring grammar/usage choices that are part of voice

## Report

Flag corrections, queries, and style-sheet updates separately. Query any change
that could alter meaning, voice, canon, or intentional dialect/register.
