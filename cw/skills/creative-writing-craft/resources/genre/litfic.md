# Literary Fiction Prose Addendum

Literary fiction rewards perception, pressure of consciousness, implication,
pattern, and sentence-level necessity. The external event matters through the
mind and life it reveals.

## Protect

- Specificity of perception and thought.
- Productive ambiguity that opens meaning rather than hiding vagueness.
- Motifs, echoes, and sentence rhythms that accumulate pressure.
- Social and emotional contradictions that resist neat resolution.

## Common LLM Failure Modes

- Mistaking vagueness for depth.
- Explaining the theme after the scene demonstrates it.
- Using lyrical language that does not change perception or pressure.
- Resolving contradiction into a tidy insight.

## During Revision

Ask what each sentence changes in attention, pressure, or understanding. Preserve
strangeness when it is exact; cut it when it is ornamental fog.
